AGNT Browser Agents — Options

This extension talks to your AGNT backend (usually http://localhost:3333) and uses your AGNT auth token. If you have multiple AGNT instances, use Detect from active tab to ensure we connect to the same one as the app you’re viewing.

Permissions

Optional permissions unlock advanced “Jarvis” capabilities (notifications, clipboard, downloads, tab/screen capture, offscreen audio). You can enable them incrementally.

To get your token: open AGNT in the browser, open DevTools Console, and run:
localStorage.getItem('token')
